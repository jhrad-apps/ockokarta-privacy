Introduction

Oƒçkokarta is a vaccination tracking app for you, your family, and your pets. We take your privacy seriously. This page explains how the app handles your data.

What data the app processes

The app allows you to store the following information:

  • Family member and pet names
  • Year of birth
  • Gender
  • Vaccination records (vaccine name, date, next dose date)
  • Notes about vaccinations

Where your data is stored

All your personal data and vaccination records are stored exclusively on your device. We do not send this information to any servers.

We do not have access to your health data and cannot see, read, or process it.

Analytics

To improve the app, we use TelemetryDeck, which collects anonymous usage statistics. This data:

  • Is not linked to your identity
  • Does not contain any personal information or health records
  • Cannot be used to track you
  • Is used solely to improve the app

Learn more about TelemetryDeck's privacy: telemetrydeck.com/privacy

What we don't do

  • We do not collect any personal data
  • We do not send your health records to any servers
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not share information with third parties
  • We do not require registration or login

Notifications

The app may send local notifications about upcoming vaccination dates. These notifications are generated directly on your device, and no information leaves your phone.

Security

The app offers optional security using Face ID, Touch ID, or a PIN code. This security protects access to the app on your device.

In-App Purchases

The app offers a one-time purchase to unlock the full version. Payments are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since all health data remains only on your device, using the app poses no privacy risk to children.

Changes to this policy

If there are changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.
